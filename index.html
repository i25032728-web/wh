A City with History and Soul

Nanchang, the capital of Jiangxi Province, is known as the "Hero City". It is the birthplace of an important moment in modern Chinese history, and today stands as a city blending culture, river life, and urban growth.

From the Gan River to historic landmarks, Nanchang offers a unique experience beyond typical tourist cities.

Four Moments of Nanchang

Morning

Walk by the Gan River and enjoy peaceful sunrise.

History

Explore Bayi Square and revolutionary landmarks.

City Life

Modern shopping streets and vibrant lifestyle.

Night

Beautiful night views along the river skyline.

The Gan River Experience

The Gan River runs through the heart of Nanchang, shaping both the landscape and the lifestyle.

It is where locals gather, relax, and experience the calm side of the city.
